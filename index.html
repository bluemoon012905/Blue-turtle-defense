Blue Turtle Defense

Build. Train. Defend.

How to Play

  • Build — Select a building from the Build tab and click a green tile to place it.
  • Towers — Arrow Towers auto-attack nearby enemies. Upgrade them with the Tech tab.
  • Barracks — Trains infantry. Pick weapons, shields, and armor from the Units tab.
  • Train Units — Click a Barracks on the map, tune the infantry loadout, then queue soldiers.
  • Tech Tree — Research upgrades in the Tech tab to strengthen your forces.
  • Wave Rewards — Each cleared wave offers 3 unlocks. Pick 1 before starting the next attack.
  • Economy — Gold Mines generate gold. Houses increase your population cap. Enemies also drop gold on death.
  • Waves — Enemies attack from the edges in escalating waves. Survive all waves to win!
  • Defend — If the Fortress reaches 0 HP, you lose.
